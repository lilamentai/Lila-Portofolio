About Me

Get to Know Me

Hamida Noor Kalila

I'm a Software Engineering student who has been exploring the world of technology for about 2 years. My journey started with UI/UX Design and Frontend Development — I loved crafting beautiful, user-friendly interfaces.

Over time, I discovered a deeper interest in Backend Development. The logic, the problem-solving, and the complexity of building systems behind the scenes fascinate me. I'm currently focused on learning JavaScript and Node.js to strengthen my backend skills.

I'm still at the beginning of my backend journey, but I bring a strong willingness to learn, a love for challenges, and a genuine enjoyment of the thinking process that comes with building software.
